The core thesis

Five structural
reasons for
reclassification

Cybersecurity's leading firms are undergoing a category transition — from discretionary software vendors to obligatory national defense infrastructure. This forces a re-pricing of their equities comparable to what Palantir experienced from 2022–2026.

01 / Policy
The government declared it
The Trump Cyber Strategy (March 6, 2026) formally classifies cyberspace as a warfighting domain. CEOs are "required to dedicate real resources" to collective digital defense. Not vendors — partners.
Doctrine shift
02 / Conflict
Iran proved the kinetic thesis
Post-Feb 28 strikes, Iranian APT Seedworm hit a U.S. bank, airport, and defense contractor. A separate attack wiped surgical equipment at hospitals globally. Cyber conflict is producing physical casualties.
Active conflict
03 / Regulation
Procurement architecture changed
CMMC Level 2 now mandatory for all CUI contracts. FedRAMP Class D (High) only capital-intensive platforms can maintain. The regulatory moat is being built by the government on behalf of incumbents.
Structural moat
04 / Paradox
CISA was hollowed out
1,000+ staff lost — 33% of the workforce. No Senate-confirmed director. The government simultaneously declared cyber a warfighting priority and dismantled its own defense agency. Private sector fills the gap permanently.
Accelerant
05 / Technology
AI breakout times collapse human defense
CrowdStrike 2026: average adversary breakout time 29 minutes. AI-enabled intrusions: 27 seconds. Human security operations cannot respond at that speed. AI-native platforms are the only viable architecture.
Tech necessity

Know the risks

When the thesis
breaks

The thesis has real failure modes. Know them before entering.

Implementation gap
High
Strategy is 7 pages with no budget line items. Contracting vehicles for offensive cyber don't exist yet. Policy → contract → revenue = 18–36 months.
CISA hollowing paradox
High
Amplifies private sector dependency but decimates coordination capacity. No director = procurement chaos risk. Watch Senate confirmation closely.
PLTR concentration risk
High
~41% government revenue creates binary political risk. Not present for CRWD/PANW/NET yet. Watch if they cross 30% threshold.
Procurement timeline
Medium
Market can price expectations before cash flows materialize. Risk of multiple expansion running ahead of fundamentals.
Geopolitical de-escalation
Medium
Iran ceasefire compresses urgency premium. China/Russia structural threat persists indefinitely — longer-term thesis remains intact.
AI disruption to existing stacks
Medium
Agentic AI could commoditize elements of current platforms. Incumbents must own the AI layer — watch Charlotte AI and Cortex differentiation.
