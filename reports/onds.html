Core thesis. Wait for price to retrace and fill the $11.00–11.28 gap, sweep the buy-side liquidity above the spike high, then confirm a bullish break of structure before committing size. The BOS is the trigger — not the gap fill. Do not anticipate.

Price returns to the gap zone created by the news spike. No position initiated — observe structure and behaviour at this level.

Break of structure confirmed
