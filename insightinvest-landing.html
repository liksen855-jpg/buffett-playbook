Folio 02 / Why it matters

Not a screener.
A research platform.

Most tools give you data. InsightInvest gives you a framework — a consistent, repeatable process for evaluating any stock the way a professional analyst would.

01
Five valuation methods, one answer
No single model is reliable in isolation. InsightInvest runs EPV, two DCF variants, owner earnings yield, and peer relative value — then combines them into a single composite fair value with a plain-English verdict.
02
Quality before price
The InsightInvestMoat™ engine scores every stock across six dimensions before you look at valuation. Buying cheap businesses is not investing — buying quality businesses at fair prices is.
03
Live data, always current
Prices, quotes, news, and financials are pulled live from Financial Modeling Prep on every search. No stale data. No cached reports from six months ago.
