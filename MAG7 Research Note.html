Exhibit 1 · Positioning backdrop

The crowded trade has moved — and it isn't done

BofA Global Fund Manager Survey, "most crowded trade." Semiconductors have never been more consensus; Magnificent 7 crowding has nearly halved since May.

Semis crowding rose 73% → 82% over three months while Magnificent 7 fell 14% → 7% — capital has been quietly rotating out of the group even as headlines stayed fixed on the AI trade. That vacated positioning is the setup for the move now underway: as an increasingly one-sided semis trade looks for an exit, a comparatively de-crowded Mag7 is the natural destination. But positioning and valuation are telling different stories here — Exhibits 2–8 show 6 of 7 names still price rich on fundamentals, even as flows rotate back in.
